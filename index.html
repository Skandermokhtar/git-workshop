Delicious Bites Menu

Appetizers

  • Caprese Salad

    Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.

  • Burrata Mushroom Bites

    These cheesy bites over whole-wheat pita rounds make for a light and fresh appetizer. Do your best to choose large, uniformly sized mushrooms to ensure you can pack an equal amount of the cheesy filling into each.

  • Sweet Pea Ricotta Spread

    Rich ricotta cheese is the perfect accompaniment to sweet peas in this vibrant spread.

Main Courses

  • Grilled Salmon

    Salmon fillet seasoned and grilled to perfection, served with lemon butter sauce.

  • Parmesan Espresso Martini Steak

    This Parmesan espresso martini steak, rubbed with coffee and served with salty fricos and lightly dressed watercress, is inspired by the Parmesan-topped espresso martini trend that was everywhere last year.

  • Grilled Tuna Steaks with Wasabi-Blueberry Sauce

    For these grilled tuna steaks with wasabi-blueberry sauce, wasabi and soy sauce add heat and umami to a fresh-cooked blueberry sauce drizzled over tuna. When cooking fish to rare or medium-rare, always use sushi-grade to reduce risk of foodborne illness.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Fondue

    Assorted fruits and marshmallows served with rich chocolate fondue.

  • Madeira Cake

    This classic British dessert is often served with Madeira wine, which is how it got its name. Similar to a pound cake, it is commonly flavored with lemon and can also be served with tea or other sweet liqueurs

  • Steamed Plum Pudding

    Plum pudding has gone through a big transformation throughout the years. It was created in the 14th century as a savory dish made of beef, mutton, raisins and prunes. It became a dessert in the 16th century and is now made with brown sugar, currants, alcohol and spices. Some recipes use figs, hence the figgy pudding we sing about at Christmas.
